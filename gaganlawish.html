About Gagan Lawish

Located in Pisoli, Pune, the Gagan Lawish is a thoughtfully planned one, equipped with all amenities for a comfortable living. The residential units in the premium Gagan Lawish project are reasonably priced within the range of Rs. 36. 0 Lac - 60. 0 Lac. The property is a part of an integrated township which spreads over an area of 4 Acre. The property comprises of 256 units which are enclosed within a peaceful environment.

A world class Residential project, it is among the finest properties available around. The available project units are in Ongoing state. The project offers property options such as Flat which are unparalleled and value for money. The spacious and well-ventilated property units vary in size from 1 BHK Flat (704. 0 Sq. Ft. - 707. 0 Sq. Ft. ), 2 BHK Flat (1042. 0 Sq. Ft. - 1187. 0 Sq. Ft. ). The project is divided into 1 towers, conceptualised to offer ease of living. The project's official launch date is 01 May 2013. 01 January 2016 is the date of possession.

Gagan Lawish Floor Plan Floor plans tell you exactly how big or small the rooms or spaces in a house are, and so are the perfect way to get an understanding of the house layoutThe properties in Gagan Lawish have 2 types of configurations and are available in 4 layouts. 1 BHK Flat comes in various sizes of 704 and 707 sq. ft super area. The aesthetically designed 2 BHK Flat is offered in attractive sizes like 1042 and 1187 sq. ft super area. The configurations include well-equipped bathrooms and balconies that allow for easy ventilation. All the various configurations of Gagan Lawish are available on Magicbricks for your reference.
