Board Of Directors

Mr. Vijay Sanghvi

The storied journey of Amco Landmark LLP started with Mr. Vijay Sanghvi, our visionary founder setting up real estate business in Pune, Maharashtra in the year 2015. Mr. Vijay Sanghvi, fondly referred to as avp of Amco Landmark LLP stated the real estate development business in Pune with a vision of plotting development and houses to home buyers at value pricing with an emphasis on quality. Mr. Vijay Sanghvi holds a degree in commerce from university of Pune. He has more than 20 years of experience in real estate development including level strategy, legal framework and safety. He believes Amco Landmark LLP has always acted like a specialized developer not an aggregator, making investments in active assets like people, processes, equipment and technology. He has been associated as promoter with the company since inception. He has more than 20 years’ experience in real estate business for business development, land procurement, funding requirements and strategy. He believes future belongs to only those developers with a brand equity built on corporate governance, innovation and execution.
