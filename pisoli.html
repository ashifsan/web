Pisoli (10lac Sqft)

Pisoli is one of the rapidly growing residential areas located off the Katraj Bypass Road in Southern part of Pune. Excellent connectivity to the various employment hubs including Magarpatta, Hinjewadi and other part of the city makes Pisoli an ideal residential destination among home buyers. Undri, Kondhwa, Camp, Hadapsar, Handewadi, Wanwadi, NIBM Road and Yewalewadi are some of the prominent neighbourhoods of Pisoli. Pushpganga Swapna Ganga, Atur Valley Vista, ARV Imperia, Nayan Mountscape and Kumar Palmcrest are some of the renowned residential projects situated in Pisoli.

Physical infrastructure

Pisoli is well-connected to other parts of the city via Mumbai-Pune Bypass and Satara Road. At a distance of 19Km from Pisoli, Pune International Airport is located, which can be covered via Airport Road. The Saswad Road Railway Station is 9Km away and can be covered through National Highway-65 whereas the Pune Junction is situated at a distance of 12Km via Kondhwa Road. Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Ltd (PMPML) buses are also available for travelling to other parts of the city.

Social & retail infra

Some of the renowned international schools such as Orchids The International School RIMS International School, Attaqwa International School and Pushpa International School are located within a radius of 5km from Pisoli. For medical assistance, residents can find Satyanand Hospital, Lifeline Hospital, Aayur Clinic and Dhanwantari Hospital and Critical Care Centre within 4km to 5km from Pisoli. Popular shopping malls like Kumar Pacific Mall, Amanora Mall and Gera Mall are situated within 9km from Pisoli.

Nearby employment hubs

Pisoli enjoys excellent connectivity to prestigious tech parks such as SP Infocity IT Park (10Km), Cybercity IT Park (14Km), EON Free Zone IT Park (15Km), Lohia Jain IT Park (18Km) and Pune IT Park (19Km). The well-known employment hubs like Kharadi (15Km) can be reached via Hadapsar Road whereas Hinjewadi (33Km) is easily accessible through National Highway-65 and National Highway-48 from Pisoli.Source: Magicbricks Research.
