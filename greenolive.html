GREEN OLIVE -

By Amco Landmark & KOLTE PATIL

When you are looking to move into a popular society, Kolte Patil Green Olive is considered one of the best around Hinjewadi in Pune. The perks of living in a society like this are many, having a for concerts and events is one of them. There is ample True in this society, your vehicle will be fully protected and safe here. Nothing beats jumping into a pool on a hot summer day, here the swimming pool is a huge hit with all the residents.

Discover the lost art of reading in this society, it is one of very few that come with a library. Working from home is convenient as this society has reliable generator back up. If you like doing some cardio, or just like to focus on weights, this society has a a gym that you should check out. Have you seen the children play zone here? If you have kids, they will love it. Access to bus stop & pharmacies is very easy & convenient from this house. With Shivam Enterprises and SelloutZone nearby, access to all your favourite stores is now a breeze. With E Square Carnival Xion, FOREVER ENTERTAINMENT Mr. Kumbhar & Wooden Pool close by, you can catch your favourite movies running & never worry about missing a show because of traffic. Blue Ridge Public School, Akshara International School and Mercedes-Benz International School are well known educational institutes in town & are very close to this home. If you are in need of any emergency services or medical assistance, you will be happy to note that Hinjawadi Hospital, Shree Hospital and Sai Hospital are very close by.
