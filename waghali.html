Wagholi (5lac Sqft)

Wagholi is a affordable locality situated in the eastern part of Pune. The pincode of this locality is 415525. This locality is near Wageshwar Nagar, Khandve Nagar and Awhalwadi. This locality has 1000+ properties to buy and 500+ properties to rent. The residents of Wagholi rated this locality at 4/5 in terms of connectivity, whereas for safety, they rated it 4/5.

Connectivity 

Kesnand Phata, is the commute option near the locality.

Infrastructure 

Presence of Care Multispeciality Hospital, Undre Hospital, Imax Multispeciality Hospital speak of sound healthcare infrastructure in & around the area.

Investment insights to Buy 

Over the last one year, Apartments in Wagholi have seen a price movement of 5.9%. The average price for buying Apartments in Wagholi is ₹ 5350.0/- per square feet. The projects that offer highest rental yield in Wagholi are Nyati Elan, Rohan Abhilasha, Kolte Patil Umang Homes. Most appreciated projects that investors can consider include Hill Shire at 41.1% YOY, Ivy Botanica at 20.9% YOY, Marvel Ganga Fria Phase 1 at 16.7% YOY. Buyers can also consider Wageshwar Nagar, Kesnand for purchasing a home as they offer property options in the same price segment as Wagholi.

Rental Insights 

The locality provides 300+ affordable properties with average rental value of around ₹16000/month, 160+ mid-segment properties with rent ranging ₹ 16000-21000/month and 130+ premium properties with rent > ₹21000/month. The average monthly rental asks in Wagholi stand at ₹7900-24300. The most popular societies in this area are Rohan Abhilasha, Kolte Patil IVY Estate and Nyati Elan.
