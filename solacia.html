About Belvalkar Solacia

Positioned at well-connected locality Wagholi, Belvalkar Solacia is an aesthetically built project of Pune. Spanning across 27 Acre, the project is well-spaced out. The project features a total of 442 units that are well-ventilated.

This skillfully crafted project has Ready To Move units. This well-designed area has a total of 4 towers, each with its own benefit. Officially this grand project's launch date is 01 October 2008. The estimated possession date of the project is 01 November 2015.

The commencement certificate of this well-planned project has not been granted. not granted is the status of the occupancy certificate of this project. The entire Belvalkar Solacia project has been helmed by renowned builder Belvalkar Housing. In Belvalkar Solacia you may take advantage of the best facilities like Power Back Up, Conference Room, Jogging and Strolling Track, Barbeque Pit, Canopy Walk, Event Space & Amphitheatre, Flower Gardens, Indoor Games Room, Indoor Squash & Badminton Courts, Kids Club. RMC Garden, Wagholi, Pune, Maharashtra is the official address of this project. This project comes under the pincode of 412207. Belvalkar Solacia is where fine living meets seamless connectivity to provide you comfort living.
